ABOUT ME

I am in my final year at the University of Toronto working towards a Specialist in Statistics and Major in Mathematics. My technical interest is in reinforcement learning, and my philosophical interest is in the metricization of human concepts.
I believe we must bridge the language-gap between us and the machine before we can meaningfully help each other.

I completed my 16-months internship at IBM as a Cloud DevOps Developer. Previous to that, I interned at Mackenzie Investments as a Technology Associate.

My best time on the job is when I apply knowledge from academia to an industry problem, optimally, it solves a problem previously thought to be impossible.
A close second is creating tools to fix inefficencies or oversights.
