Dinner Recipe: Whatever was in my fridge Lamb Stew
Cut (double the size of your fist) amount of lamb shank into 1 square inch cubes, put inside ziplock bag and marinate with:
2 tablespoons of salt
4 tablespoons of black pepper
8 tablespoons of paprika
1/4 cup of light-soy-sauce
1/4 cup of dark rum
Leave in fridge overnight

Put inside a stew-pot:
1 whole white onion (diced)
6 cloves of garlic (diced)
6-8 cherry tomatos (halved)
2 tablespoons of coconut oil
Cover the top surface with a sprinkle of salt
Put on medium-high heat until golden-brown

While this browns prepare:
1 whole potato (diced) 1 whole carrot (diced)

After contents of the stew-pot has turned golden-brown:
Add in 4 tablespoons of tomato paste, stir until homogeneous and cover for 5 minutes
After tomoto paste mixture has been heated, add in the diced potatos and carrots
Sear lamb chunks until surface is almost black
Add lamb chunks to the now boiling stew
Reduce heat to low, let lid cover half the pot, simmer for 3 hours
Serve with steamed white rice
